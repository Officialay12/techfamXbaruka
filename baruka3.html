Baruka

The Shadow Monarch's Loyal Warrior

Baruka is one of the most formidable warriors under the command of the Shadow Monarch. Known for his unwavering loyalty and immense strength, he is a key figure in the battle against the forces of darkness. His presence on the battlefield strikes fear into the hearts of his enemies, and his shadowy aura is a testament to his power.

As a shadow soldier, Baruka embodies the essence of the Shadow Monarch's army. His combat skills, combined with his ability to manipulate shadows, make him a force to be reckoned with. Whether it's protecting his allies or annihilating his foes, Baruka is a true warrior of the shadows.
